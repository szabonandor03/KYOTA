This campaign applies the four `[SYSTEM: ROLLOUT_RULE_*]` playbooks from the KYOTA wiki directly to FIDESZ SAPKA's current situation: a 3-person Hungarian hip-hop group with a politically charged name, 3 songs released, and zero social media push on the latest track.

## Strategic Assessment

### What You Have
- A **provocative name** that is impossible to ignore in the Hungarian context — this is your greatest marketing asset and it's free.
- A **3-song catalog** on all streaming platforms — enough to prove you're real, not enough to be old news.
- A **newest track with zero promotion** — this is actually an advantage. You haven't burned the moment yet.

### What You Need
- Awareness and initial discovery (nobody knows you exist yet beyond your circle).
- A reason for a stranger to click play.
- Content that spreads *without* you having to beg people to listen.

## Phase 1: The Provocative Signal (Days 1–3)
**Rule Applied: `ROLLOUT_RULE_HIDDEN_HAND` + `ROLLOUT_RULE_NARRATIVE_LABOR`**

The name FIDESZ SAPKA is inherently viral in Hungary. Don't explain it. Don't apologize for it. Let it do the work.

### Actions:
1. **The Cold Drop Post** — Post a single image on Instagram/TikTok with:
   - The group name in stark, aggressive typography (apply `UI_IMPLEMENTATION_RULE_TYPOGRAPHIC_RHYTHM` — treat the letters as graphic objects, stack them, make them loud)
   - No explanation. No "new song out!" caption. Just the name and a streaming link in bio.
   - Caption: something ambiguous or deadpan. A single emoji, a date, or a line from the track with zero context.

2. **The Comment Bait** — The name alone will provoke reactions ("is this political?", "WTF is this?", "is this a joke?"). **Do not clarify.** Every confused comment is free algorithmic engagement. Apply the DOOM rule: if the World is strong enough, explanation is optional.

3. **Short-Form Video (TikTok/Reels):** A 15-second clip that:
   - Shows the 3 of you but **doesn't show faces clearly** (shot from behind, silhouettes, masked, in shadow). Apply the Hidden Hand — make them curious about WHO before they care about WHAT.
   - Uses the hardest 10-second section of the new track as audio.
   - Zero text overlay. Zero hashtags in the video itself.

## Phase 2: The Content Engine (Days 4–10)
**Rule Applied: `ROLLOUT_RULE_MORAL_ECONOMY` + `ROLLOUT_RULE_NARRATIVE_LABOR`**

Now that the initial signal has provoked curiosity, start feeding the algorithm without ever looking desperate.

### Actions:
4. **Behind-the-Scenes Scraps:** Post raw, 10-15 second studio clips. Not polished content — literal phone recordings of the session where the track was made. The **Aesthetic of Labor** (Frank Ocean rule) — show the work, not the product. People invest in process.

5. **The "Street Typography" Series:** Create 3-4 visual posts using the track's best lyrics rendered in aggressive, Reid Miles-inspired typographic layouts. Each post is a single bar or punchline, treated as a graphic object. These are shareable standalone content that doubles as promotion.

6. **Engage the Political Confusion:** If people comment asking if you're political, respond with lyrics from the song (not explanations). This is the **Narrative Ambiguity** rule from Gorillaz — refuse to define it, let the audience attach their own meaning.

7. **Cross-Pollinate with Gyuris:** Have Gyuris share/repost one piece of FIDESZ SAPKA content. His existing audience (however small) is your closest warm lead. This is a one-time signal boost, not a sustained campaign.

## Phase 3: The Social Proof Loop (Days 11–21)
**Rule Applied: `ROLLOUT_RULE_HISTORICAL_ARTIFACT`**

### Actions:
8. **Pin Your Best Performing Post.** Whatever got the most engagement from Phase 1-2, pin it. New profile visitors see the strongest signal first.

9. **The "Catalog Card" Post:** Create a single, clean visual showing all 3 tracks as a numbered catalog (apply the **FAC Numbering** rule from Factory Records). Present it as:
   ```
   FS-001: [Track 1 name]
   FS-002: [Track 2 name]  
   FS-003: [Track 3 name] ← YOU ARE HERE
   ```
   This frames you as an ongoing project with a growing archive, not a one-off joke.

10. **User-Generated Bait:** Post a TikTok with the audio and a simple challenge or meme format that's easy to replicate. The track becomes a **tool** for other creators, not just something to passively listen to.

**Rule Applied: `ROLLOUT_RULE_HIDDEN_HAND`**

## Rules to Never Break

> [!CAUTION]
> - **Never beg for listens.** No "please check out our new song 🙏" posts. Ever. The Daft Punk rule: the persona is above desperation.
> - **Never over-explain the name.** The ambiguity IS the brand. The moment you explain it, you lose the mystique.
> - **Never post without visual intent.** Every image must pass the Vignelli test: is this grid-aligned, is the typography deliberate, does white space have purpose? Bad graphics kill credibility faster than no graphics.
