- `log.md` is the append-only state ledger and fallback mailbox for KYOTA.
- Appending one structured record to the end of this file is always allowed. Rewriting earlier records or the contract section is a hotspot edit and requires its own `CLAIM`.

- The newest unresolved `BLOCKER` for a claim remains active until a matching `UNBLOCK`, `HANDOFF`, or `RELEASE`.

```text

```
