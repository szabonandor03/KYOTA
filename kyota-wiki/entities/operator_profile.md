## FIDESZ SAPKA: Visual Identity & Brand Philosophy
- **Identity Elasticity:** The band's visual identity must remain flexible, avoiding the trap of being boxed into a single "lo-fi" aesthetic. It should be capable of handling raw, documentary-style VHS footage just as easily as experimental or high-concept visuals. Contrast is key.
- **Authenticity over Algorithm:** Reject forced, generic marketing tropes (e.g., standard "link in bio" or obvious "Street Typography" posts) in favor of content that feels "real" and true to the group's natural dynamic.
- **Appropriation & Retrieval:** Leveraging existing iconic imagery (e.g., the pixelated Google Drive triangle for "adide" and "király") to create a raw, subverted aesthetic.
