# Creative Selection: Prototyping & Iteration

**Date:** 2026-04-18
**Source URIs:**

## Technical Core
KYOTA adopts the "Creative Selection" model of software engineering for any UI or experiential feature. The primary unit of progress is the tangible prototype, not the architectural specification.
1. **Demo-Driven Development:** Do not over-index on dry theoretical planning for interactive elements. Produce minimum-viable UI demos as early as possible.
2. **Taste and Heuristics:** Use established design heuristics ("taste") to immediately judge and refine the prototype rather than waiting for external specifications.
3. **Continuous Convergence:** Accept that initial versions will be flawed. Feature development is a mechanism of continuous iteration where the best variations are selected, merged, and polished over strict cycles.
